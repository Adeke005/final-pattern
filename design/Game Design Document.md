Game Summary
  Genre: Tower Defense
  Platform: Desktop (libGDX)
  Core Mechanic: Place towers and defend against waves of enemies
Controls
  Left Mouse Button (LMB): Place a tower
  Right Mouse Button (RMB): Remove a tower
  ESC: Pause the game
Player (Base)
  HP: 100
  Gold: Starting amount — 200 
Enemies
  BasicEnemy: Slow and weak
  FastEnemy: Fast but fragile
  TankEnemy: High HP, slow movement
  BossEnemy: Appears at the final wave
Towers
  ArrowTower: Fast attacks, low damage
  CannonTower: Slow attacks, high damage
  IceTower: Slows enemies
   Towers can be upgraded up to 3 levels.
   Each upgrade increases damage, attack speed, and range.
   Players can merge two identical towers to create a stronger advanced tower.
   Merged towers have unique abilities and higher stats.
Levels
  Different level maps 
  Enemies follow a predefined path
Win / Lose Conditions
  Win: All waves are completed
  Lose: Base HP reaches 0
Art Style
  Simple shapes or pixel art
